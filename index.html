F*** Mondays

...if you are doing the same thing everyone else is doing.

When we were kids, we were honest about our fantasies. We were hungry for knowledge and all that mattered was fun. Later, we grew up and started doing what we were told to. Soon, we started despising Mondays, and continued with our lives without trying to make our days count.

We honestly believed that this is not the road we were meant to take, because if you are not truly happy, it will show on products you deliver. We wanted an environment where we would feel free to talk about ideas, a place where we could learn something new every day and where we could come to be productive and give our maximum. We wanted a place where you would ask yourself: “How come the whole week has already gone by?!”.

So we founded a company and brought our friendship to the office. We didn’t want employees; we wanted a family of professionals who could devise killing solutions. We called it StuntCoders.

StuntCoders acts as a family, and when we accept a new project, our client becomes a part of our family. We want to make our clients feel welcome, and we want them to know that we care. We care about their ideas and plans and try hard to make them happen. We understand how vital their vision is, as we have fulfilled ours.

Our knowledge is our client’s shortcut to success. And we don’t search for shortcuts in our work. We believe in people and ideas. We know that with a good idea, we can do more, and we’re ready to work more and harder than others because we know that, in the end, we’ll be proud. Proud of what we achieved, proud of ourselves and proud of our work.

Dejan Jacimovic

How Does It Work?

I will try to explain how it works in a simple three-step process. =)

STEP 1

Discovery Call

Discovery call is organized with your tech lead to understand what kind of team they need so that we can match them with candidates. Once we understand your needs and wants, we'll have a good understanding of the problems you need to be solved. Then we sign the contract and move to step 2.

STEP 2

Networking and Sourcing

I attend developer events and often meet people from different areas of problem-solving. By meeting them through conferences and meetups superficially, I can drill down their knowledge further and recognize which team they'd be an excellent fit for. I put together developers with developers, and only those I know are highly motivated to change their job.

If I cannot reach developers through my network, I do sourcing by researching Open Source projects on GitHub or other measurable metrics to find the best fit for your team.

STEP 3

Only High-quality Leads

I go to work managing your expectations and delivering on our promise to provide you with the best talent possible. You go to work with a piece of your mind knowing the right, available talent is coming your way to work with your team and on your projects.

This way, we avoid bad hires and short-term matches.

Frequently asked questions

Security?

We use shared password management tools like LastPass to access your email without seeing your passwords. We also connect via Virtual Private Networks (VPNs) to secure our devices when working on your email.

Privacy?

Privacy is critical to us. Your Recruiting Manager has already gone through several stages of vetting by us can sign any additional NDAs or DPAs you provide.

Cancellation?

Contracts are month-to-month, and each month is prepaid. This way, you can request to cancel your subscription at any time. If you decide to cancel, keep the candidates' database at no extra cost.

Cost?

Our pricing is based on how much work is required to manage your recruiting workload. Your monthly fee may go up or down based on how much help you need.
