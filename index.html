How Does It Work?

I will try to explain how it works in a simple three-step process. =)

STEP 1

Discovery Call

Discovery call is organized with your tech lead to understand what kind of team they need so that we can match them with candidates. Once we understand your needs and wants, we'll have a good understanding of the problems you need to be solved. Then we sign the contract and move to step 2.

STEP 2

Networking and Sourcing

I attend developer events and often meet people from different areas of problem-solving. By meeting them through conferences and meetups superficially, I can drill down their knowledge further and recognize which team they'd be an excellent fit for. I put together developers with developers, and only those I know are highly motivated to change their job.

If I cannot reach developers through my network, I do sourcing by researching Open Source projects on GitHub or other measurable metrics to find the best fit for your team.

STEP 3

Only High-quality Leads

I go to work managing your expectations and delivering on our promise to provide you with the best talent possible. You go to work with a piece of your mind knowing the right, available talent is coming your way to work with your team and on your projects.

This way, we avoid bad hires and short-term matches.
