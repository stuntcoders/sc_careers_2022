Our

Interview Process

Meeting you is always an exciting moment for us, and we would like to make sure you feel comfortable when interviewing with us. The steps below outline what you can expect:

  • Step 0

    An online test takes about 45 minutes to one hour to determine the essential knowledge for the position

  • Step 1

    30 min informal conversation to get to know each other.

  • Step 2

    A 1-hour conversation to dive deeper into your technical skills and explore your fit with the required culture.

  • Step 3

    A 1-hour coding challenge or assessment where you get the chance to work alongside some of your potential teammates.

  • Step 4

    A final conversation during which we tackle any remaining questions on both sides.

We do every interview in pairs and with different assessors because we would like you to meet more team members. It also gives us the chance to eliminate biases.
